# Helpful Links

Resources for voter registration, ID, and the SAVE Act.

---

## Utah State Resources

- **[vote.utah.gov](https://vote.utah.gov/)** - Utah's official voter information portal with registration tools, election dates, and voting guides
- **[VoteSearch Utah](https://votesearch.utah.gov/voter-search/search/search-by-voter/voter-info)** - Look up your voter registration status and details
- **[Utah Voter Registration Portal](https://secure.utah.gov/voterreg/index.html)** - Register to vote or update your existing registration online
- **[Securing Your Mail Ballot](https://vote.utah.gov/securing-your-mail-ballot/)** - Information about signature verification and ballot security
- **[Utah Driver License Division](https://dld.utah.gov/)** - Apply for a state ID card (free for eligible indigent voters)
- **[Voter ID Requirements](https://vote.utah.gov/voter-id-requirements/)** - Official list of acceptable IDs for voting in Utah

## Salt Lake County Resources

**Contact:** (385) 468-7400 | vote@saltlakecounty.gov | 2001 S State St, Suite S1-200, SLC 84114

- **[Salt Lake County Elections](https://www.saltlakecounty.gov/clerk/elections/)** - County clerk's election homepage
- **[Check Voter Registration (SL County)](https://www.saltlakecounty.gov/clerk/elections/register/check-voter-registration/)** - Verify your registration in Salt Lake County
- **[Cure Letter Information](https://www.saltlakecounty.gov/clerk/elections/cure/)** - What to do if your ballot signature is flagged
- **[Update Your Address](https://www.saltlakecounty.gov/clerk/elections/register/update-address/)** - Update your address if you've moved within the county

## Salt Lake City

SLC elections are administered by Salt Lake County. The city maintains a voter info page:

- **[SLC Voter Information](https://www.slc.gov/attorney/voter-information/)** - City-maintained page consolidating registration links and voting guidance

## Federal Resources

- **[vote.gov](https://vote.gov/)** - Federal voting information and state-by-state registration links
- **[USA.gov Voter Registration](https://www.usa.gov/voter-registration)** - General information about registering to vote
- **[SAVE Act (H.R. 22)](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/house-bill/22)** - Full text of the current bill in Congress

## Document Requests

- **[travel.state.gov](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/passports.html)** - Apply for or renew a U.S. passport
- **[VitalChek](https://www.vitalchek.com/)** - Order birth certificates from most U.S. states
- **[USCIS](https://www.uscis.gov/)** - Request replacement naturalization certificates

## Free ID Assistance

- **[VoteRiders](https://www.voteriders.org/states/utah/)** - Nonpartisan nonprofit offering free help obtaining voter ID (866-432-8683)
